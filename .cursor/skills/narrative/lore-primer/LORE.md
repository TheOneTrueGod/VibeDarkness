## Player-facing framing [Draft]

The product is a **shared online lobby** where groups pick games to play together; **Minion Battles** is one of those games and carries most authored fantasy content (missions, cards, units). This doc does **not** lock cosmology or a single metanarrative—it only anchors what the repo already implies so agents do not contradict it casually.

## What belongs in this file vs elsewhere [Draft]

- **Here:** Cross-cutting facts, timeline beats, and “how the setting hangs together” at a **high** level—still with stability tags, not exhaustive lists.
- **Style slices:** Naming, tone, and format live under `writing-style-* / STYLE.md`.
- **Game data:** Specific enemies, abilities, and dialogue live in code and data files; this primer should **reference themes**, not duplicate rosters.
